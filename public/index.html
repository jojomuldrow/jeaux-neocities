Welcome to my web diary!

welcome to my neocities site! i created this site because i've always enjoyed coding and i miss when ppl had blogs instead of social media. i don't really have a precise plan in place for this site, other than it being my "passion project" and a place to dump all fruits of my creative endeavors. anyway, thank you for visiting the site! feel free to write a message in the guestbook at the bottom of this page! xoxo

About Me

my name is Jeaux, pronounced like "joe," but my (current) lover calls me Kitten Doll (hence the site name) ^_^ i use any pronouns & i'm genderfluid! my bday twin is Janet Jackson (May 16th!) so i'm a Taurus :D i'm from Inglewood, CA so ykwtfgo. i created this website to showcase my full range of expression! my biggest dream is to be a respected musician, songwriter, dancer, producer, & vocalist. gotta dream big lol ultimately, i want to be "renaissance" personified. some of my past/current hobbies are art, web design (obv), astrology, tattooing, makeup, TS4/TS2, terraria, 420, reading, & a whole buncha other sh*t.
